About EcoVision Solar Placement

EcoVision Solar Placement leverages YOLO-based object detection models (.pt) alongside solar algorithms and Gemini AI for recommending optimal solar panel placement. Our goal is to assist users in maximizing solar efficiency using intelligent automation.

We use YOLO (You Only Look Once) models (.pt files) for object detection, combined with solar angle calculations and Gemini AI for smart recommendations.
